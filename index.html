The Holland Project’s Billboard Gallery showcases the work of exceptional emerging and established regional artists on billboards throughout Reno’s surface streets. Three new artists are installed every four weeks.

Henry MacDiarmid was battle born in Reno, Nevada where he currently lives and works. He graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Studio Art with an emphasis in Photography in 2018 from the University of Nevada, Reno. He has exhibited in solo and group exhibitions at the Holland Project in Reno, Nevada from 2015 on. He helped organize and curate for the Reno, Nevada gallery, window mine from 2020 through 2022.
