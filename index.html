He has exhibited in solo and group exhibitions at the Holland Project in Reno from 2015 on. He helped organize and curate for the Reno gallery, window mine from 2020 through 2022.

Statement

Poetry

North Valleys Library | 1075 N Hills Blvd, Reno, NV 89506
May 15 - July 15, 2023 | Reception: June 21, 2023 @ 5PM-6:45PM

It is a funny thing to title an art show “Poetry.” Is “Poetry” the best one word poem? Poe it tree. Maybe since the art show is in a library, that has something to do with it. Poetry is one of the hardest easy things to do, like making a readymade. Combining words and phrases almost like out of thin air. Someone must have said that the world is a stage. Maybe it’s a page? In a magazine? On a screen? Sometimes your words may not be your own. Thinking about the right words to say, it’s hard to remember your lines. React, as if they were written for you to spell out. Silent, as if what you see is best left unexplained. Every word could be the last. Impact, cut to black.

Spoilers
Star shaped cardstock prints that are placed into novels as bookmarks. The bookmarks are placed in books categorized as “dystopian” through the Washoe County Library’s catalog search available to check out at the time of install. One side of the bookmark quotes the ending of the book it is placed into. The other side is a photograph cropping an advertisement of people smiling that was marketed during March 2020 - March 2022. Why did it seem like the only thing that did not stop during the pandemic was advertising?
